What We Do

Geometry that
nature already solved.

Bridging the gap between biological architecture and manufacturable implant geometry — no mesh intermediates, no guesswork.

Triply Periodic Minimal Surfaces →

Gyroid, Schwartz-P, Lidinoid, Fischer-Koch S — fully parametric implicit scaffolds with tunable porosity and stiffness anisotropy.

Bioinspired Composite Architecture →

Helicoidal fiber layouts inspired by mantis shrimp dactyl club and cortical bone — directional stiffness hierarchies in a single isotropic material.

Homogenization & Validation →

FFT-based mechanical homogenization with physiologically validated strain targets (500–1000 µε) for bone scaffold applications.

Open-Source Pipeline →

Browser-based TPMS builder at mshomper.github.io/tpms-builder — no proprietary lock-in, full transparency, export-ready.

Topology Optimization →

Load-path driven lattice infill strategies, field-guided grading, and structural optimization for ALIF cages, acetabular cups, and more.
